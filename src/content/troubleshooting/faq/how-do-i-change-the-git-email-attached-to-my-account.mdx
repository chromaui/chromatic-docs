# How do I change the git email attached to my account?

To change your git email attached to your account, you'll need to do the following:

1. Open the [Notifications page](https://www.chromatic.com/notifications).
2. At the bottom of the page, you'll find the git email address we have on record. Press `Change`.
3. Edit the email and press `Update email`.
