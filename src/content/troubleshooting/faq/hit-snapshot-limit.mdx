# What happens when I use all included snapshots?

Once you use all of your **included snapshots**, the outcome varies depending on your account type.

For free accounts, review and testing will be paused until your included snapshots reset.

For paid plans, reviews and testing will continue uninterrupted, and you will be billed for any additional snapshots used within the current billing period.

Learn how to set usage alerts to track additional snapshot usage [here](/docs/billing#usage-alerts).
