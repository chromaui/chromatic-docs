# How can I change my Chromatic plan?

To change your Chromatic plan, you'll need to do the following:
1.  Select `Billing` on the left side menu.
2. Press `Update Plan`.
3. Choose the plan you would like to switch to and press `Change`.

For `Enterprise Plans`, please use our in-app chat to get in touch, or email us at support@chromatic.com.
