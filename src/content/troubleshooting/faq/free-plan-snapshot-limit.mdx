# What happens when I use all included free snapshots?

Free plans include 5000 snapshots per month. Once you use all included free snapshots, testing and review will pause until the next month, when Chromatic will begin taking snapshots again and functionality will automatically resume. Upgrading to a paid plan will immediately resume testing and review.
