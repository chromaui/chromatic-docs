# What's the difference between Figma in Chromatic and Figma Plugin?

[Figma in Chromatic](/docs/figma-in-chromatic) brings designs into Chromatic for side-by-side comparison with stories, while the [Figma Plugin](/docs/figma-plugin) embeds live stories within Figma.
