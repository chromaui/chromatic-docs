# How do I rename a project?

To rename a project, you'll need to do the following:

Navigate to the project and click the `Manage` tab on the left-hand side

Click on the `Configure` tab

Select the `Rename` option under `Projects`

Please note that to rename your project, you must enter a unique project name.
